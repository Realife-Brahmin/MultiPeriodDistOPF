IEEE 123-Bus 5-POI System - Zone Bus Assignments
=================================================

Zone 1 (Substation 1s - SW-Residential):
  Buses: 1-39
  Key buses: 3, 7, 9, 11, 18, 22, 26, 30, 34, 35, 37

Zone 2 (Substation 2s - NW-Commercial):
  Buses: 40-53
  Key buses: 41, 47, 48, 50, 53

Zone 3 (Substation 3s - N-Industrial):
  Buses: 54-79, 122-128
  Key buses: 58, 61, 62, 67, 71, 73, 75, 79

Zone 4 (Substation 4s - NE-Office):
  Buses: 80-106, 109-121
  Key buses: 84, 87, 92, 94, 97, 101, 106

Zone 5 (Substation 5s - SE-Charging):
  Buses: 107-108, 111-116
  Reserved for future EV charging infrastructure expansion

Notes for Inkscape Figure:
- Zone 1: Southwest quadrant (left-bottom cluster)
- Zone 2: Northwest section (upper-left, buses 40-53)
- Zone 3: Central/North spine (middle section extending upward)
- Zone 4: Northeast quadrant (right-upper cluster, buses 80-106)
- Zone 5: Southeast section (right-bottom, buses 107-116 reserved)
